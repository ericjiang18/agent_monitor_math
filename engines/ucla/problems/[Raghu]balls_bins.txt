Consider the following random process. There are n balls in n bins ordered left to right, and distributed arbitrarily. In each step a random ball is chosen and all of the balls in that bin are moved one bin to the left  if any; if it is the left-most bin then the balls disappear from the process. Show that the expected time for all balls to disappear is at most O(n^{2-c}) for some c > 0.
