Let G_n = F_q^n for q an odd prime. For a function f:G_n->[0,1], define E(f) to be the average value of f on G_n and Lambda(f) = sum_{x,d in G_n} f(x)f(x+d)f(x+2d). 

Problem. Prove or disprove the following statement. For any alpha in (0,1], there exists a positive integer m such that for all sufficiently large integers n, if f:G_n -> [0,1] minimizes Lambda(f) subject to the constraint E(f) \geq alpha, then there is a subspace W of G with codimension at most m such that f is constant on cosets of W.
